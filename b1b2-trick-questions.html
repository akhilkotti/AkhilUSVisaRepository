B1/B2 Visa – TRICK QUESTIONS & SAFEST ANSWERS

(For Mr. Nanduri Vijaya Bhaskara Rao & Mrs. Nanduri Aruna Kumari)

TRICK AREA 1: LENGTH OF STAY

“Why only three weeks? Why not longer?”

SAFE ANSWER:
I have official duties to resume in India. I have permission only for three weeks.
✔ Shows employment obligation
❌ Never say: “If visa allows, we may stay longer”

“What if your sons ask you to stay longer?”

SAFE ANSWER:
I cannot. I must return to India as per my job responsibilities.
✔ Strong return intent

TRICK AREA 2: IMMIGRATION INTENT

“Do you plan to settle in the U.S. with your sons?”

SAFE ANSWER:
No. I am permanently settled in India. This is only a short family visit.
✔ Clear non-immigrant intent
❌ Never say: “If opportunity comes…”

“Will you apply for a Green Card later?”

SAFE ANSWER:
No. I have no such plans. My career and life are in India.
✔ Ends suspicion immediately

TRICK AREA 3: SPONSORSHIP & FINANCES

“Why are your sons inviting you?”

SAFE ANSWER:
It is a personal family visit to spend time together.
✔ Emotional but non-immigrant

“Can your sons support you financially if needed?”

SAFE ANSWER:
We are financially independent. They are only providing accommodation.
✔ Avoids dependency risk
❌ Never say: “Yes, they will take care of everything”

TRICK AREA 4: EMPLOYMENT PRESSURE TEST

“What happens if you don’t return on time?”

SAFE ANSWER:
That is not possible. I am a government employee and must report back as scheduled.
✔ Very strong answer (Govt job = high trust)

“Can someone else handle your work while you are away?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Only temporarily. I must return to resume my responsibilities.
✔ Temporary absence confirmed

TRICK AREA 5: FAMILY TIES IN INDIA

“Do you have anyone dependent on you in India?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Yes. We have family, relatives, property, and social responsibilities in India.
✔ Anchors you to India

“Why don’t your sons visit you instead?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Their work and studies make it difficult, so we are visiting briefly.
✔ Logical and reasonable

TRICK AREA 6: FUTURE PLANS (VERY IMPORTANT)

“What will you do after returning from the U.S.?”

SAFE ANSWER (Mr. Rao):
I will immediately resume my duties with the Andhra Pradesh Police Department.

SAFE ANSWER (Mrs. Kumari):
I will return home and continue my family responsibilities.
✔ Crystal-clear future in India

TRICK AREA 7: PREVIOUS TRAVEL HISTORY

“Have you traveled abroad before?”

SAFE ANSWER (if yes):
Yes, and I always returned within the permitted period.

SAFE ANSWER (if no):
No, this will be my first international visit.
✔ Honesty is critical

DANGEROUS ANSWERS (NEVER USE BELOW WORDS)

❌ “We will see how things go”
❌ “If my sons need me, I may stay”
❌ “They want us to help them settle”
❌ “We might extend if allowed”
❌ “Maybe apply for dependent visa later”

GOLDEN RULE (MEMORIZE THIS)

“Temporary visit + Strong India ties + Fixed return date”
