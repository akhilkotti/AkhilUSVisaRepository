Canada Visa at Your Doorstep - VFS Global

In 2026, VFS Global (the official partner for IRCC) offers a "Visa at Your Doorstep" service in India. This is designed for people who want to avoid traveling to the Visa Application Centre (VAC).

step-by-step process for a "home-delivered" experience.

The Two-Part Digital & Physical Process

Part 1: The Online Application (Mandatory)

Regardless of home collection, the initial application must be submitted online first.

  1. Submit Online: Log into the IRCC Portal and upload your documents (Invitation letter from daughter, bank statements, etc.). Submit your application on the IRCC Portal .
    Note: You should Sign in to the IRCC portal and complete the process. If you dont have an account Signup.For more details on how to fill the application, click on the link Visa Guidance .

a. Personal & Financial Documents (From You - The Applicant)

  • Current & Old Passports: Scanned copies of the bio-data page (first and last) and all pages with previous visa stamps or entry/exit marks.

    Note: Combine your current passport's front/back pages and all stamped pages from old passports into one PDF.
  • Financial Proof (Your Assets):
    • Last 6 months of bank statements (signed/stamped by the bank).
    • Income Tax Returns (ITR) for the last 3 years.
    • If you are still working: Salary slips (last 3 months) and an NOC/Leave letter from your employer.
    • If you have Business: Include your Business Registration to show strong ties to India.
    • If you are retired: Pension statements or proof of retirement.
  • Ties to India (Proof of Return):
    • Property documents:If you own a home or land or commercial space in Hyderabad, include the registration or tax receipts.
    • Fixed Deposits (FDs) or other investments.
    • A "Purpose of Travel" letter explaining that you are visiting family and intend to return to your home in Hyderabad.
  • Digital Photo: 35mm x 45mm, white background, in JPG format (the portal has a specific cropping tool).

b. Documents from your DAUGHTER (The Host)

  • Invitation Letter: A signed letter from her stating:
    • Your full name and birth date.
    • Her address and phone number in Canada.
    • The purpose and duration of your visit.
    • A promise that she will support your stay (housing/food).
  • Proof of PR Status: A clear scan of the front and back of her Canadian PR Card or her Record of Landing (COPR).
  • Proof of Relationship: Your birth certificate or your daughter’s birth certificate (showing the applicant name(Father/Mother)).
  • Financial Documents (Her income):
    • Her most recent Notice of Assessment (NOA) from the Canada Revenue Agency.
    • Her recent pay stubs and an employment letter.

c. Forms (Completed Directly in the Portal)

The IRCC Portal is now mostly "form-less," meaning you type your info directly into the website rather than filling out PDFs. However, you will still answer questions that cover:

  • IMM 5257: Application for Visitor Visa.
  • IMM 5645: Family Information (details about your parents, siblings, and children).

d. Specific Checklist for "Super Visa" (If you choose that)

If you decide to go with the Super Visa (to stay up to 5 years at a time), you must add:

  1. Medical Insurance: Proof of a paid 1-year private medical insurance policy from a Canadian insurance company (minimum $100,000 coverage).
  2. Medical Exam: You may be asked to do a "Upfront Medical Exam" with an IRCC-approved doctor in Hyderabad (like at Apollo or Ankura Hospitals).
Pro-Tip for Hyderabad Applicants:

When you upload your bank statements, ensure they are in a single PDF file. The portal has a 4MB limit per document. If your bank statements are long, use a "PDF Compressor" online to reduce the size before uploading.
  1. Pay Fees: Upload the documents above. You will pay $100 CAD for the visa and $85 CAD for biometrics (Total: $185 CAD).
  2. Wait for the BIL: Within 24–48 hours, you will receive a Biometric Instruction Letter (BIL) in your portal. You need this letter to use the doorstep service.

Part 2: The Doorstep Service (The "Home Collection" Phase)

Once you have your BIL, you can opt for the Visa at Your Doorstep service.

  1. Book the Service: For Canada, VFS does not use a "one-click" booking system for doorstep services in India. Instead, you must follow this manual request process:

1. The Correct Page for Canada (India)

Do not use the global "On-Demand" link. Instead, go to the official VFS Canada-India page:

  • Link: https://visa.vfsglobal.com/ind/en/can/
  • On this page, click on "Additional Services" and look for "Visa At Your Doorstep". If the "Book Now" button is missing or redirects you to a standard calendar, you must use the Email/Phone method below.

2. How to Book for Hyderabad (The Manual Way)

Since you are in Hyderabad, the mobile team will likely be dispatched from their Banjara Hills office. Because they need to coordinate equipment and a team, you must request a quote first:

  • Step A: Complete your online visa application and wait for your Biometric Instruction Letter (BIL). You cannot book this service without the 13-digit number on that letter.
  • Step B: Send an email to the VFS premium service team or call their helpline.
  • Helpline: +91 22 67866002 (This is the general Canada-VFS helpline for India).
  • What to ask: "I have my BIL and I want to book the 'Visa At Your Doorstep' service for an individual in Hyderabad. Please provide the current fee and the inquiry form."
  • Step C: They will send you a specific PDF form or a link to a private inquiry page. Once you fill this out, a Customer Service Manager will call you within 24 hours to give you a quote (likely between ₹15,000 and ₹20,000 for Hyderabad) and a payment link.

3. Alternative: Premium Lounge (The Middle Ground)

  • Cost: ₹4,767 (much cheaper than doorstep).
  • Why choose it: You still have to go to the office, but there are no queues, you get personalized assistance, and you can book this directly through the standard appointment link you found earlier by selecting "Premium Lounge" as the appointment category.
Summary Recommendation:

If you strictly want the team to come to your home, call the helpline or use the "Contact Us" web-chat on the VFS Canada site. They do not allow "instant" booking for Canada doorstep services because they must verify the availability of the mobile biometric kits in Hyderabad first.
  1. VFS Coordination: A VFS team member will contact you to confirm your address, check your documents over the phone, and schedule a visit.
  2. The Home Visit:
    • Biometrics: A mobile team will come to your house with a portable kit to take your fingerprints and digital photo.
    • Document Collection: They will collect your physical passport, the BIL, and the signed VFS Consent Forms.
  3. Passport Submission: They will securely transport your passport to the Canadian High Commission.
  4. Home Delivery: Once the visa is approved, VFS will use their Two-Way Courier Service to deliver your stamped passport directly back to your home.

Important Things to Know

  • Availability: This service is available in major cities like Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc. If you live in a remote area, they may charge extra for travel.
  • Two-Way Courier: Even if you don't use the full "Doorstep" service, you can always choose the Two-Way Courier specifically for the final stage (passport stamping) so you don't have to visit the center twice.

Summary of Steps to Start

  1. Finish your online IRCC application today.
  2. Get your BIL (Biometric Letter).
  3. Go to the VFS website and click "Book Now" for the Doorstep service.
