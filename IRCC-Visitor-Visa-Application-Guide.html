IRCC Visitor Visa Application Guide

Once you have logged into the IRCC Portal, the system will guide you through a series of questions that "build" your application checklist.

Since you are visiting your daughter in Canada, here is the exact sequence of what happens next:

Step 1: Start the Application

  1. On the main dashboard, click "Apply for a visitor visa, study or work permit."
  2. Review the "Checklist of documents" page and scroll to the bottom to click "Start Application."

Step 2: The Online Questionnaire (Building Your Profile)

The portal will ask a series of questions.

Answer them as follows to ensure you get the correct document checklist:

  • "Why do you need a visa?" Choose: To visit family or friends.
  • "What are you visiting Canada for?" Choose: To visit my child or grandchild.
  • "How long do you plan to stay?" (This is crucial: If you want the Super Visa to stay for years, choose More than 6 months. If you want a standard visitor visa, choose Less than 6 months).
  • "Are you applying for a Super Visa?" Select Yes or No based on your choice.
  • "Where do you live?" Choose: India.

Step 3: Personal Details & Background

You will then move through several "tabs" of information:

  • Personal Information: Enter your name, date of birth, and passport details exactly as they appear in your passport.
  • Employment & Education: Provide details of your work history (Technical Project Manager/Software Engineer) for the last 10 years.
  • Travel History: List all countries you have visited in the last 5 years. Since you already have a Canada visa, mention your previous visits to Canada.
  • Family Information: You will enter details about your spouse, parents, and all children (including your daughter in Canada and your daughter in Canada/Canada-based family).

Step 4: Uploading Documents

Once the questionnaire is done, the portal will show a Document Checklist.

This is where you upload the scans we discussed:

  1. Passport: Scan the bio-data page and all stamped pages into one PDF.
  2. Funds: Upload your bank statements and ITRs.
  3. Invitation Letter: Upload the letter from your daughter.
  4. Host’s PR Status: Upload her PR card copy.
  5. Proof of Relationship: Upload your daughter's birth certificate showing your name.
  6. Ties to India: Upload your property papers or business registration.

Step 5: Consent and Declaration

  • You will be asked to sign a digital declaration.
  • You do not need to print it; you simply type your name exactly as it appears in the system to "e-sign" the application.

Step 6: Payment

  • The final step before submission is the payment page.
  • Fee: $185 CAD (includes the visa fee and biometrics).
  • You will need a credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) or a prepaid credit card to pay.

What Happens After You Click "Submit"?

  1. Confirmation: You will receive a "Submission Confirmation" message in your portal.
  2. BIL (Biometric Instruction Letter): Check your portal again after 24 hours. You should see a PDF letter called the BIL.
  3. The Home Collection Call: This is when you go to the VFS Global website to book the "Visa at Your Doorstep" service for Hyderabad. They will ask for your BIL number to schedule the visit to your home.
