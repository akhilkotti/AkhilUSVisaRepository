B1/B2 Visa Interview Preparation

For Chinmai – Wedding Visit (August 2026)

Interview Questions and Answers

1. Why are you going to the USA?
Answer:
To attend my uncle’s daughter’s wedding.
2. When is the wedding?
Answer:
on August 19, 2026.
3. Who is inviting you?
Answer:
My uncle, Mr. Akkiraju Venkata Satya Kiran Kumar is inviting.
4. How are you related to him?
Answer:
He is my aunt’s husband. I am the son of his wife’s younger sister.
(Very important: This must match the wordings in invitation letter.)
5. Where will you stay in the USA?
Answer:
I will stay at his residence.

If asked for address:
2616 Lynwood Drive, Troy, Michigan, 48083.
6. How long will you stay?
Answer:
Stay for three weeks.
7. When will you return?
Answer:
In first week of September 2026.
8. Who will pay for your trip?
Answer:
My father is sponsoring my travel expenses
9. Your parents and brother also applied. Are you going together?
Answer:
We have applied separately and have interviews on different dates, but we are going for the same family wedding.
10. What do you do in India?
Answer:
I work as a Functional Consultant at ADP Private Limited in Hyderabad.

If asked Since when?:
I have been working there since 14 NOVEMBER 2023.
11. What is ADP?
Answer:
ADP is a global payroll and HR services company.
12. Will you work in the USA?
Answer (Always):
No. I am going only for the wedding and will return after the scheduled stay.
13. Do you have relatives in the USA?
Answer:
Yes, my uncle and his family live there.
Do NOT hide this. Hiding relatives causes rejection.
14. Why will you return to India?
Very important question.

Answer:
I have a permanent job at ADP in Hyderabad. My career is in India.
15. Have you been to any other countries?
No, this will be my first international trip.

Key Focus for Approval

  • Strong reason → Wedding
  • Strong ties with India → Job/Family
  • Clear return plan
  • Honest relationship

Interview Behavior (Very Important)

  • Dress neatly (formal/semi-formal)
  • Speak clearly
  • Be honest
  • Short answers
  • No over-explanation
  • Eye contact
  • Confident tone
Don’t say:
  • I may stay longer
  • I will look for work
  • Maybe I will settle

  • Copy of U.S. Passport (Bio Page)
