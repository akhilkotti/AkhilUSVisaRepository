PART 8: KEY INTERVIEW TIPS

↑ Back to Features

✔️ DO

  • Be confident and calm
  • Answer in simple English
  • Be honest
  • Keep answers short
  • Carry documents neatly
  • Maintain eye contact

❌ DON’T

  • Do not say “I will work”
  • Do not say “Maybe I will stay”
  • Do not give long stories
  • Do not contradict DS-160
  • Do not appear unsure

GOLDEN RULE FOR B1/B2 BUSINESS VISA

Your answers must clearly show:

  • Temporary Visit
  • Training Only
  • No Employment
  • Indian Salary
  • Strong Return Plan

If any of these are missing → risk of rejection.

SAMPLE PERFECT SUMMARY ANSWER (If Asked Open-Ended)

Tell me about your trip?

I am working as a Civil Engineer at SEATS in Hyderabad. I am traveling to OTHON headquarters in Texas for a three-month internal training program. The purpose is to learn advanced engineering systems and quality processes. I will remain on Indian payroll and will not work on projects. After completing training, I will return to India and implement these practices in our operations.
