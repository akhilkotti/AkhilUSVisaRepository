B1/B2 Visa Appointment Checklist

Attending Wedding in USA

1. VAC (Biometrics) Appointment

a. Passport/s - All Applicants

  • Current passport + old passports which has US Visa Stamping (if any)
  • Mandatory

b. DS-160 Confirmation Page/s

  • With barcode
  • Printed
  • Mandatory
Note: During biometrics the VAC officer will stamp the DS-160 confirmation page.

c. Appointment Confirmation

  • Visa Interview Confirmation showing VAC & Consulate schedule
  • Printed copy
  • Mandatory

d. MRV Fee Receipt

  • Printed copy
  • Optional, But good to carry

2. Consulate (Visa Interview) Appointment

a. Passport/s - All Applicants

  • Current passport + old passports which has US Visa Stamping (if any)
  • Mandatory

b. DS-160 Confirmation Page/s

  • DS 160 Confirmation Pages with original VAC stamp
  • Mandatory
Note: Carry the stamped DS-160 confirmation received during biometrics.

c. Appointment Confirmation

  • Visa Interview Confirmation showing VAC & Consulate schedule
  • Printed copy
  • Mandatory

d. MRV Fee Receipt

  • Printed
  • Optional, But good to carry

e. Wedding Invitation & Event Proof

  • Wedding invitation card (If Available, Good to have)
  • Invitation letter from Invitee, inviting you to attend his Son/daughter's wedding
  • Copy of Invitee's U.S. Passport (Proof of U.S. Citizenship)
  • Copy of Invitee's address proof in USA (Driving License / Utility Bill if available)
Note: Invitation letter should mention the wedding date, venue and relationship with the applicant.

f. Relationship Proof

  • Birth certificate of the applicant showing parents’ names
  • Birth certificate of Invitee's showing same parents’ names (if available)
  • Family relationship proof such as:
    • Aadhaar cards showing family details
    • Any government document showing family relationship, Only if available
  • Copy of Invitee's U.S. Passport (Citizenship Proof)

g. Financial Evidence

  • Bank statement - Financial Statement from the bank with bank seal/signature.
  • IT returns - Printed copies of Income Tax Returns for the last 3 years
  • Salary slips - Printed copies of salary slips for the last 3 months
  • online balance confirmation certificate.
Note: Make sure that your bank shows funds a minimum of 6 lakhs.

h. Ties to India

  • Job offer letter - Original copies on company letterhead (with seal/signature)
  • Property / Business Documents - In the absence of a Chartered Accountant–issued financial statement, the applicant should carry the original business registration papers for verification.
  • Family proof - Original and printed copies of Aadhaar cards for all visa applicants

Carry all original documents and copies to avoid delays in visa processing.
