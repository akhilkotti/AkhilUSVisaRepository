America First in Family Values

U.S. Consulate General Kolkata | New Priority Visa Categories

The U.S. Consulate General in Kolkata has launched a significant new initiative as of May 2026 aimed at streamlining the visa process for families. This program, titled "America First in Family Values," introduces specialized priority appointment slots specifically for parents.

The "Parents" Priority Category

This category addresses the historically long wait times for visitor visas in India. By segregating applicants into specific pools, the consulate aims to expedite interviews for those with clear, verifiable ties.

  • Eligibility: Parents 50 years of age or older.
  • Target Group: Those visiting children legally residing in the U.S. (e.g., on work visas like H-1B or L-1, or student visas like F-1).
  • Current Availability: Exclusive pilot at the Kolkata Consulate. Applicants must select "Kolkata" as their interview location.

Other New Priority Streams in Kolkata

Alongside the parent category, three other specialized "lanes" have been introduced:

1. Business Professionals: Dedicated slots for verified business travelers to support trade and investment links.
2. General Tourism (Clean Record): For applicants with no history of prior visa refusals.
3. Recent Visa Refusals: A specific track for those refused within the last 24 months, involving a unique internal review process.

Practical Advice for Applicants

  • Location Strategy: Travelers from other parts of India may choose Kolkata for an interview to benefit from shorter wait times, provided Kolkata is selected on both the DS-160 and the U.S. Travel Docs portal.
  • Documentation: Standard B1/B2 requirements apply. Officers will focus on ties to India (property, family, pension, or business income).
  • Verifying Legal Status: Present clear copies of the child's U.S. status (I-797, valid visa, or I-20) as verified reasons for travel are core to this group.

If successful, these categories may expand to Chennai, Hyderabad, Mumbai, or New Delhi.

DS-160: Selecting the Location

On the DS-160 (CEAC website), the location is selected at the beginning of the process.

Where to select "Kolkata"

  1. The Start Page: Visit the CEAC website. Locate the drop-down: "Select a location where you will be applying for this visa."
  2. Selection: Choose "INDIA, KOLKATA" from the list.
  3. Security Check: Complete the captcha and click "Start an Application" to generate your ID.

Important: Portal Matching

The "Parents" category is specific to the Kolkata system. To ensure visibility of slots:

  • Consistency: The location selected on the DS-160 must match the interview post selected on the U.S. Travel Docs (Atlas) portal.
  • Visibility: Specialized categories only appear for users who have designated Kolkata as their post in their booking profile.

Updating an Existing DS-160

If your submitted DS-160 is for another city:

  1. New Application: Start a new DS-160 on CEAC, selecting "INDIA, KOLKATA".
  2. Retrieve & Copy: Use the "Retrieve an Application" feature to import data from your previous form.
  3. Update Portal: Log in to U.S. Travel Docs, enter the new DS-160 number, and set the "Post" to Kolkata.
Note: While rules generally allow bringing a DS-160 confirmation page from one city to another, for these priority categories, it is strongly recommended that the DS-160 lists Kolkata to avoid technical or verification issues.
