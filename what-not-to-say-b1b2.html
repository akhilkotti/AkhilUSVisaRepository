What NOT to Say in a B1/B2 Visa Interview

(Even If the Officer Asks Directly)

❌ 1. “If my sons ask me to stay longer, I will”

Why this fails:

Confirms immigrant intent

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

I cannot stay longer. I must return to India as per my approved leave.

❌ 2. “We will extend the visa if allowed”

Why this fails:

Shows pre-planned overstay

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

We will return within the permitted stay.

❌ 3. “My sons will take care of everything”

Why this fails:

Shows financial dependency

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

We are financially independent. Our sons are providing accommodation only.

❌ 4. “We want to help our sons settle”

Why this fails:

Implies long-term stay

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

This is only a short family visit.

❌ 5. “We may apply for another visa later”

Why this fails:

Future immigration intent

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

We have no such plans.

❌ 6. “We will decide after reaching the U.S.”

Why this fails:

No fixed return plan

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

Our return date is fixed.

❌ 7. “My sons asked us to come”

Why this fails:

Shifts control to U.S. residents

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

We decided to visit them for a short time.

❌ 8. “We might help with childcare / house work”

Why this fails:

Unpaid work = visa misuse

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

We are visiting only to spend family time.

❌ 9. “We want to see if life in the U.S. suits us”

Why this fails:

Trial immigration intent

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

We are settled in India.

❌ 10. “If health permits, we may stay longer”

Why this fails:

Conditional stay

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

Our stay is limited to three weeks.

❌ 11. “Our sons will apply Green Card for us”

Why this fails:

Permanent immigration intent

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

There are no such plans.

❌ 12. “We will take care of our grandchildren”

Why this fails:

Long-term caregiving intent

✅ SAFE ANSWER:

This is a short personal visit only.

❌ 13. Asking Questions That Hurt You

Never ask the officer:
  • “Can we extend after entering?”
  • “How long can parents stay?”
  • “Can we convert visa type?”

OFFICER PSYCHOLOGY (IMPORTANT)

Visa officers assume:
Every Visitor applicant is an intending immigrant UNTIL proven otherwise
Your job:
✔ Prove temporary intent
✔ Prove strong return obligations
✔ Prove financial independence

ONE-LINE SAFE UNIVERSAL ANSWER

If you feel trapped, say:
“This is a short family visit. We will return to India as planned.”

  • Government employment
  • NOC
  • Mandatory return
As long as you avoid the red-flag statements above, approval chances are very high.
