Canada Visitor Visa Cost Estimate

10-Year Application Cost Breakdown | Hyderabad, India

For a 10-year Visitor Visa application from Hyderabad, here is the total estimated cost breakdown. Since you already have a valid visa, you have two main paths depending on whether you want to "reset" your 10-year biometrics clock.

1. Mandatory Government & VFS Fees

These are the base costs for your application:

Fee Component Cost (CAD) Cost (INR Approx.)
Visa Processing Fee $100 ~₹6,150
Biometrics Fee (Optional but Recommended*) $85 ~₹5,230
VFS Service Fee — Included in Biometrics fee
Total Mandatory Fees $185 ~₹11,380
*Note: If you don't pay the $85 biometrics fee, your new visa will only be valid until your current biometrics expire (10 years from when you last gave them).

2. Optional "Visa At Your Doorstep" (Hyderabad)

Estimated Doorstep Service Fee: ₹15,000 – ₹20,000
(In addition to government fees)

What it includes: A VFS team comes to your location in Hyderabad with a mobile biometric kit, captures your fingerprints/photo, and collects your documents and passport in one visit.

3. Other Small Costs (Optional)

Two-Way Courier ₹1,716 (Passport pickup and delivery later)
SMS Alerts ₹179 (Application status updates)
Premium Lounge ₹4,767 (Faster, comfortable service at VFS Hyderabad)

Grand Total Estimates

Standard Route (Visiting VFS Hyderabad in person):
Total: ~₹11,500 – ₹12,000
(Includes visa fee, new biometrics, and basic courier)

Premium Doorstep Route (VFS comes to you):
Total: ~₹28,000 – ₹32,000
(Includes visa fee, new biometrics, and home service premium)

Summary of the Process

Apply Online
Pay the $100 (and optional $85) on the IRCC website first.
Wait for BIL
You will receive a Biometric Instruction Letter (BIL) via email.
Book VFS
Once you have the BIL, book an in-person appointment at the Hyderabad VFS office (Banjara Hills) or the "Visa At Your Doorstep" service.
Insurance
For your trip, a 30-day medical policy ($100k coverage) for age 58 will cost roughly ₹10,000 – ₹15,000 total (paid later).
