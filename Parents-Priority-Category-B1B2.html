Parents Priority Category (B1/B2)

Official Initiative: "America First in Family Values" – U.S. Consulate Kolkata

The Parents Priority category is a specialized initiative launched by the U.S. Consulate in Kolkata as of May 2026. It is designed to drastically reduce wait times for elderly parents who want to visit their children in the United States.

1. Key Eligibility Criteria

To book under this category, the applicant must meet three specific conditions:

  • Age: The applicant (parent) must be 50 years of age or older.
  • Purpose: The primary reason for travel must be to visit a child residing in the U.S.
  • Child's Status: The child in the U.S. must be there legally (e.g., on a valid H-1B, L1, F1-OPT, or as a Green Card holder/Citizen).

2. Primary Benefit

  • Priority Slots: Applicants who select this category get access to a dedicated pool of appointment slots that are much earlier than the general B1/B2 pool.
  • Wait Time: While general visitor visa wait times in India often stretch into late 2026 or 2027, the Kolkata Consulate has been releasing these priority slots for dates as early as May and June 2026.

3. How to Access It

This is not a separate visa class; it is a scheduling category within the standard B1/B2 application process.

  • Location Selection: You must select Kolkata as your interview location on the usvisascheduling.com portal.
  • Category Choice: When prompted to select an appointment type, look for the option labeled "Parents visiting children with legal status" or similar wording provided by the Kolkata post.
  • Verification: You should be prepared to provide proof of the child’s legal status in the U.S. (like a copy of their visa, I-797, or Green Card) and proof of relationship (birth certificate) during the interview.

4. Important Restrictions

  • Kolkata Only: As of today, this facility is exclusive to the Kolkata Consulate. It has not yet been implemented in Chennai, Hyderabad, Mumbai, or New Delhi.
  • Standard Rules Apply: Even with a priority slot, the applicant still needs to demonstrate "strong ties" to India and convince the officer that they intend to return after their visit.
Note: If you are booking for a parent who meets these criteria, it is highly recommended to shift the interview location to Kolkata even if you reside in a different part of India, as the time savings are currently significant.

Step-by-Step Scheduling Guide

You should select Kolkata at the very beginning of the appointment scheduling process on the usvisascheduling.com portal. Specifically, this selection is made on the "Post Selection" screen, which appears immediately after you have completed your profile and paid the MRV fee.

1. The Post Selection Screen (Crucial Step)

After you log in and click "Schedule Appointment," the first page will ask you to select your Post (Embassy or Consulate).

  • Action: You must select Kolkata here.
  • Why: These priority categories are "post-specific." If you select Chennai or Hyderabad, the "Parents visiting children" category will not appear later in the workflow.

2. Selecting the Visa Category

Once Kolkata is selected, you will proceed to the "Visa Priority" or "Visa Category" section.

  • Selection: Choose Non-Immigrant Visa.
  • Sub-category: Look for the specific label that says something like "Parents visiting children with legal status" or "B1/B2 - Parents Priority."

3. The Calendar View

If you have correctly selected Kolkata and the specific parent category, the calendar that appears will show the priority dates (currently as early as May/June 2026).

Important Checklist for Success

  • DS-160 Location: It is a common myth that your DS-160 location must match your interview location. You can use a DS-160 that was originally filled out for "Hyderabad" or "Chennai" to book an appointment in Kolkata.
  • Biometrics (VAC) vs. Interview: Remember that you will need to book two appointments. You can actually do your Biometrics (VAC) in a different city (like Hyderabad) and only travel to Kolkata for the actual Consular Interview.
  • Age Verification: Ensure the applicant is strictly 50 years or older. If you book this slot for someone younger, the guards at the Consulate may turn them away on the day of the interview.
Pro-Tip: If you have already started an application and selected a different post, you may need to click "Close and Start New Application" on the portal dashboard to reset the Post Selection screen and choose Kolkata. Your paid fee will remain linked to your profile and will not be lost.
