US Visa Interview Preparation Guide

This guide is prepared for Vijay Kumar Pulgari and Sailaja Pulgari based on their DS-160 applications for a B1/B2 Visitor Visa.

Purpose of travel: Visiting their son Raj Kumar Pulgari in New Jersey and attending his graduation ceremony at Stevens Institute of Technology on May 27.

Applicant Profile Summary

Applicant Occupation Monthly Income Purpose of Visit
Vijay Kumar Pulgari Business Owner – Centering Works / Interior Works ₹1,00,000 Visit son & attend graduation ceremony
Sailaja Pulgari Landlady / Property Management ₹55,000 Visit son & attend graduation ceremony

Most Important Purpose of Visit Answer

Officer: Why are you going to the United States?

Best Answer:

We are visiting our son in New Jersey and attending his graduation ceremony at Stevens Institute of Technology on May 27.
This is a strong and clear travel purpose because:
  • Specific family event
  • Time-bound travel
  • Strong reason for short visit
  • Fits perfectly under B1/B2 visitor visa

Important Interview Tips

  • Keep answers short and direct
  • Maintain eye contact
  • Do not over-explain
  • Answer confidently
  • Ensure answers match DS-160
  • Speak naturally and calmly

Answers to Avoid

1. “We may stay longer”(Never tell)

Better:

We will stay for two weeks.

2. “We want to settle in the US”(Never tell)

Better:

We are visiting only for the graduation ceremony and family visit.

3. “We are looking for opportunities”(Never tell)

Better:

We will return to continue our business and responsibilities in India.

Ideal 20-Second Interview Example

Officer: Why are you going to the US?

attending our son graduation ceremony.

Officer: How long will you stay?

For two weeks.

Officer: What do you do in India?

I run a centering and interior works business in Hyderabad.

Officer: Your visa is approved.

Golden Rule

The visa officer mainly wants to understand:
  • Why you are going
  • How long you will stay
  • Why you will return to India

If these answers are clear, simple, and confident, approval chances improve significantly.
