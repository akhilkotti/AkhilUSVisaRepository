B1/B2 Visa – TRICK QUESTIONS & SAFEST ANSWERS

(For Mr. Vijay Kumar Pulgari & Mrs. Sailaja Pulgari)

TRICK AREA 1: PURPOSE OF VISIT

“Why are you going to the United States?”

SAFE ANSWER:
To attend our son’s graduation ceremony at Stevens Institute of Technology in New Jersey.
✔ Clear, short and genuine purpose

“Which university is your son studying at?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Stevens Institute of Technology in New Jersey.
✔ Specific and confident answer

“What course is your son studying?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Computer Science.

“When is the graduation ceremony?”

SAFE ANSWER:
On May 27.

TRICK AREA 2: LENGTH OF STAY

“How long will you stay in the U.S.?”

SAFE ANSWER:
For two weeks.
✔ Short and reasonable stay
❌ Never say: “We may extend our stay”

“What if your son asks you to stay longer?”

SAFE ANSWER:
We cannot stay longer as we need to take care our business and family responsibilities in India.
✔ Strong return intent

TRICK AREA 3: IMMIGRATION INTENT

“Do you plan to settle in the United States?”

SAFE ANSWER:
No. We are permanently settled in India. This is only a short family visit.
✔ Clear non-immigrant intent
❌ Never say: “If opportunity comes…”

“Will you apply for a Green Card later?”

SAFE ANSWER:
No. We have no such plans. Our business, family, and life are in India.
✔ Ends immigration suspicion quickly

TRICK AREA 4: FINANCES & SPONSORSHIP

“Who is paying for the trip?”

SAFE ANSWER (Vijay Kumar):
I am paying for the trip myself.

SAFE ANSWER (Sailaja):
My husband is paying for the trip.

“What is your monthly income?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Vijay Kumar: Around ₹1 lakh per month.

Sailaja: Around ₹55,000 per month.
✔ Must match DS-160 exactly

“What is your annual income?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Vijay Kumar: Around ₹12 lakhs per year.

Sailaja: Around ₹6.5 lakhs per year.

TRICK AREA 5: PREVIOUS VISA REFUSAL

“Have you ever been refused a U.S. visa?”

SAFE ANSWER:
Yes. Our previous B1/B2 visa application was refused under Section 214(b).

“Do you know why your visa was refused previously?”

SAFE ANSWER:
There may have been some confusion regarding our income details and family travel details during the previous interview. We have now provided clear and accurate information in the current application.
✔ Professional and respectful answer
❌ Never blame the previous visa officer

TRICK AREA 6: STRONG TIES TO INDIA

“Why will you return to India?”

SAFE ANSWER:
We have our business, rental properties, family, and responsibilities in Hyderabad.
✔ One of the most important answers

“What will you do after returning from the U.S.?”

SAFE ANSWER (Vijay Kumar):
I will continue my civil and interior contracting business in Hyderabad.

SAFE ANSWER (Sailaja):
I will continue managing our rental properties and family responsibilities.
✔ Strong future plans in India

TRICK AREA 7: TRAVEL HISTORY

“Have you traveled abroad before?”

SAFE ANSWER:
No, this will be our first international visit.
✔ Honest and simple answer

DANGEROUS ANSWERS (NEVER USE BELOW WORDS)

❌ “We may stay longer”
❌ “We will see opportunities there”
❌ “Maybe we will settle later”
❌ “If visa allows, we may extend”
❌ “Our son wants us to stay permanently”

GOLDEN RULE (MEMORIZE THIS)

“Short visit + Strong India ties + Fixed return plan”
