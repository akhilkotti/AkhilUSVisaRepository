US Visa Dropbox Eligibility Guide

Part 1: Before Fee Payment (Basic Setup)

When you Start Application, the system first collects only basic details:

  • Purpose: Business/Tourism
  • Visa Class: B1/B2
  • Passport details
  • DS-160 number
  • Age / previous visa info

At this stage:

  • ❌ Dropbox is NOT decided yet
  • ✔️ Only your profile is created

Then → You are taken to payment.

Part 2: After Fee Payment (Final Dropbox Decision)

After your MRV fee is activated:

You click Schedule Appointment

Now the system (controlled by the
U.S. Department of State) shows:

The Real Eligibility Questions:

  • Previous US visa?
  • Same category?
  • Refusal?
  • Valid/expired?
  • Biometrics?
  • Overstay?
  • Details changed?

These are the actual Dropbox filter questions.

Result Screen (Important)

After answering:

If Eligible ✅

You’ll see:

Congratulations! You qualify for an Interview Waiver

Dropbox calendar opens

If Not Eligible ❌

You’ll see:

You must attend interview

Interview slots appear

Key Point (Many People Get This Wrong)

You DO pay first,
then Dropbox is decided.

  • ✔️ Payment ≠ Dropbox guarantee
  • ✔️ Answers = Decision

That’s why answering correctly is critical.

My Advice

When you reach Part 2:

  • Don’t rush
  • Read every question
  • Answer truthfully
  • No guessing

PART-2: Final Dropbox Eligibility Questions (After Fee Payment)

These appear after payment, when you click Schedule Appointment, under the system of the
U.S. Department of State.

Question 1

Have you been issued a U.S. visa in the past?

Answer: YES

✔️ Because you already have B1/B2

Question 2

Is your previous visa still valid or expired within the last 48 months?

Answer: YES

✔️ Yours is valid till Nov 2026

Question 3

Are you applying for the same visa class as your previous visa?

Answer: YES

✔️ B1/B2 → B1/B2

Question 4

Was your last visa cancelled, revoked, or marked ‘Cancelled Without Prejudice’?

Answer: NO

✔️ Only say YES if embassy cancelled it (rare)

Question 5

Has your previous visa ever been refused?

Answer: NO

✔️ You already confirmed this

Question 6

Have you ever overstayed, violated status, or worked illegally in the U.S.?

Answer: NO

(Only if 100% true)

Question 7

Have your personal details changed since your last visa?

(Name / DOB / Nationality / Gender)

Answer: NO

(Unless legally changed)

Question 8

Were you fingerprinted at your last U.S. visa interview?

Answer: YES

✔️ Almost everyone was

This is cross-checked with your DS-160. You should have answered Yes for the question, Have you been ten-printed? in DS 160.

Question 9

Is your previous visa lost, stolen, or damaged?

Answer: NO

✔️ You still have it

Question 10

Are you applying in your country of nationality or residence?

Answer: YES

✔️ India → India

FINAL RESULT (What You Should See)

If you answer like above, system will show:

Congratulations! You qualify for an Interview Waiver.

  • Dropbox calendar opens
  • No interview
  • Only VAC submission

TOP 5 Mistakes (Avoid These)

  • ❌ Clicking NO for previous visa
  • ❌ Saying details changed (when not)
  • ❌ Saying fingerprint = NO
  • ❌ Rushing through questions
  • ❌ Using wrong DS-160
